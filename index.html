Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette cobines beaten eggs cooked to perfect, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
